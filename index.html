Activities

Support and Campaigns

We coordinate blood donations, organize support circles, and assist with academic growth.

Ongoing

Welfare Programs

Providing financial guidance, basic educational supplies, and support for students experiencing crisis during semesters.

Weekly

Student Support Activities

Mentorship circles matching senior students in SQA with juniors, assisting in career guidelines and exam prep.

24/7 Hotline

Blood Donation Campaigns

Maintaining a central blood donor database of SQAT club volunteers to assist emergency requests from students and DIU families.

Annual

Volunteer Programs

Bootcamps designed to teach volunteer communication, leadership qualities, teamwork, and program organization skills.

Events

Community Service

Extending care outside the DIU walls via clean campus initiatives, orphanage visits, and basic tech literacy drives.
